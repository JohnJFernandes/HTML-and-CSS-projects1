ADVENTURE AWAITS YOU IN ALASKA

Please come experience the opportunity of a lifetime!The first leg of your journey is to get to Juneau AK, which can easily be reached via Seattle WA or from many other cities, an alternate is flying to Sitka AK. Once you have arrived, you jump aboard a float plane for a scenic 60 mile flight S. Base camp is one of the first areas they discovered gold in AK, a settlement from over 150 years ago with relics and remains abound. From there we will kayak, hike, fish, watch wildlife, hunt, relax, take a nap or a sauna and eat some of the best seafood of your life. We can tailor an adventure to maximize your time in the largest temperate rainforest in North America.
